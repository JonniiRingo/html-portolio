Mirdian Lab

Meridian is an interactive data-analysis lab that lets you drag-and-drop raw files, pipe them through a configurable stack of transforms, and watch complex, high-dimensional patterns crystallise in real time. From noise-gating and wavelet denoising to CCDM invariant extraction and curve-fitting, every step is visual, undoable, and GPU-accelerated. The result: clearer insight, faster iteration, and a friction-free path from messy numbers to actionable models.
